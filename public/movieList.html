My Top 3 Best Movies of All Time

Aliens: Romulus

Aliens: Romulus brought the franchise back to its core beginings. A true si-fi horror movie that kept me on the edge of my seat and deeply entertained

Fight Club

Fight Club is both a cinema and literary classic. Touching on sensitive topics of mental health and depression long before these words were even in the main stream. "It's only after we've lost everything, that we are free to do anything"

Erased (Yes it's acutally an anime show)

My final entry into to my top 3 is a bit odd, but wathcing Erased felt more like watching a movie with its unusual intro and outro cuts (probably a mistep in bringing the movie into an english speaking demograph) the story is powerful and the ending twist was one that makes you think you knew what was happening and quickly humbles you. I can not recommend this one enough, and its an easy watch for non anime lovers also.
